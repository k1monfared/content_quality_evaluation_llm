Privacy note.

      
Stored privately: your email, the passage
      text, the chosen judge model, and the returned scores are stored in the operator's own
      private database. They are used only to run this demo, to return a cached result if the
      same passage and model were evaluated before, and to enforce the cost limits. They are
      not shared or made public.

      
Sent to the model provider: to produce a score,
      the passage text and the judge prompt are sent to the selected model's provider through
      Poe. Poe receives the passage and the prompt but nothing about you, not your email and
      not your identity.

    

    

    
    


    

  


  

    This demo is separate from the study pipeline. Scores come from a live judge
    model call through a rate-limited proxy. The rubric, per-model prompts,
    composite weights, and threshold are the finalized study values. The good or
    bad decision is driven by the direct overall score at the study threshold of
    6, and the composite is the study's refined fitted composite over neutrality,
    verifiability, coverage, and readability.
